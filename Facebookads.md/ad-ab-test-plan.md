# FB/IG Ad A/B Test Plan (30 Days)

## Objective Priority
1. Lead form submissions (primary)
2. WhatsApp click starts (secondary)
3. Calls from ads (secondary)

## Budget Split (Starter)
- Total: $35/day
- Prospecting: $25/day
- Retargeting: $10/day

## Audience Sets
- **A1 Local Movers:** Liverpool + 20km, renter + moving interests
- **A2 Airbnb Hosts:** Western Sydney, short-stay/hosting interests
- **A3 Small Biz Offices:** office managers + local business owners
- **R1 Retargeting:** 30-day website/video engagers

## Weekly Testing Rules
- Run 2 creatives per ad set per week
- Minimum 1,500 impressions each before decisions
- Pause any ad with CPL > 1.5x account average after day 4
- Scale winner by +20% budget every 48h if CPL stable

## Pass/Fail Targets
- CTR (link): >1.5%
- CPL: <$22
- Cost per WhatsApp start: <$10
- Frequency: <2.5 on prospecting
