# Clean Up Bros — Daily Social Ops Checklist

## Daily (AM)
- Check yesterday’s FB/IG spend, leads, CPL, bookings.
- Reply to all comments/DMs from last 24h.
- Post 1 local proof asset (before/after, testimonial, or availability).
- Verify all active ads are approved + delivering.

## Daily (Midday)
- Speed-to-lead audit: confirm response time under 15 min.
- Qualify new leads (suburb, service, timeframe, property size).
- Follow up warm leads older than 24h.

## Daily (PM)
- Post story sequence (availability + social proof + CTA).
- Log top objections seen today.
- Update KPI tracker (leads, qualified %, bookings, CPL, cost/booking).

## Twice Weekly
- Creative review: pause worst 20–30% by CPL.
- Produce 3 new hooks from real objections.
- Refresh retargeting urgency copy.

## Weekly (Friday)
- Run one A/B test decision: winner/loser documented.
- Reallocate budget to top 2 performing ad sets.
- Export weekly report snapshot for management.
