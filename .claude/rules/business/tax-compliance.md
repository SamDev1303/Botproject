# Australian Tax Compliance

> Critical rules for bookkeeping and BAS preparation
> Last updated: 2026-02-02

## GST Rules

- **Rate:** 10% (calculate as Total ÷ 11)
- **Registration threshold:** $75,000+ annual turnover
- **Clean Up Bros:** Registered for GST (ABN 26 443 426 374)

## BAS Worksheet Labels

- **G1:** Total sales (GST inclusive)
- **G10:** Capital purchases (equipment, vehicles)
- **G11:** Non-capital purchases (supplies, fuel, etc.)
- **GST on Sales:** G1 ÷ 11
- **GST Credits:** (G10 + G11) ÷ 11
- **GST Owing:** GST on Sales - GST Credits

## Tax-Free Threshold

- **Individual:** $18,200/year
- **Below threshold:** No tax payable
- **Above threshold:** Progressive tax rates apply

## Superannuation

- **Rate:** 11.5% of gross employee wages (mandatory)
- **Payment:** Quarterly (due 28th after quarter end)
- **Contractors:** Not required (ABN holders)

## Record Keeping

- **Invoices:** Keep for 5 years
- **Receipts:** Keep for 5 years
- **Bank statements:** Keep for 5 years
- **BAS lodgements:** Keep records forever
