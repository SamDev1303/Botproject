# Documentation Templates

## SHIFT NOTE STRUCTURE
- PRESENTING SITUATION / OVERVIEW (Client state, emotional regulation, risk level)
- CHRONOLOGICAL SUMMARY (Time-blocked activities and behaviors)
- RISKS / SAFETY / BEHAVIOUR (Bullet points of specific risks + rating)
- SUPPORTS & INTERVENTIONS (Emotional and practical supports provided)
- OUTCOME (State at shift conclusion + handover details)

## INCIDENT REPORT STRUCTURE
- ANTECEDENTS (Triggers/stressors before the event)
- WHAT HAPPENED (Chronological facts only, no interpretation)
- ACTIONS TAKEN (Specific de-escalation steps, emergency contacts)
- SERVICES CONTACTED (Police, Ambulance, Supervisor)
- OUTCOME (Final condition, risk status, staff injury)
- FOLLOW-UP (Clinical reviews, behavior support updates)

## VIVA ENGAGE STYLE
- Brief, 1-paragraph summary
- Mood, engagement, medication compliance, notable behaviors.
